Our free public health education is an essential component of promoting healthy behaviors and preventing the negative health impact of technology. It aims to provide individuals and communities with the knowledge, skills, and resources to make informed decisions about their health and wellbeing in relation to the current technological age. A free public health education program can play a crucial role in ensuring that everyone has access to this information, regardless of their socio-economic status or geographic location. Such a program can cover a wide range of topics, including making decicion in purchasing of the latest devices, living with them, safe usage of the devices and available protective measures.

It can be delivered through various mediums, such as online courses, workshops, webinars, podcasts, and social media. By providing free and easily accessible information, a public health education program can empower individuals to take control of their health when interacting with these devices. Furthermore, a free public health education program can benefit not only individuals but also the larger community. By promoting safe usage measures and protective options for devices,  it can reduce healthcare costs, increase productivity, and improve the overall quality of life as these devices have a huge impact on our life.
